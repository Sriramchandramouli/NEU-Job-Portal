Frequently Asked Questions

We get lots of mail and always want to answer your inquiries. That's why our staff has created this mini FAQ page for the most commonly asked questions. Just click on any question to display the answer inline on the page.

Website Interface

How do I log into the website interface?

You need to have a valid account to login to our website and perform search operation on the jobs.

Where can I find resources for learning?

The Home page has a link for blogs. The blogs page have plenty of articles and you can make the best use of it

How Does the Search Feature Work?

We have an optimized search feature for searching jobs. You will be notified if employer views your resume.

User Accounts

Can I have more than one user account?

Nope, sorry. You need to be a Northeastern employee or student to access this website

How do I edit my profile?

You have Settings menu in the home page. Clicking it will take you to User Account page, where you can edit the profile.

What are the various user roles?

The employers have the ability to post jobs and also view the resumes submitted by the candidates. The job seekers can upload the resume, search for jobs and then apply to them.

Spam & Support

How can I get in touch with Staff Support?

You can either send a Feedback. Otherwise, there is a contact us option in home page, through which you can e-mail us.

Will I be able to delete all my account data?

You can deactivate and delete account.

Is there a limit to the job applications per day?

No, there's no limit.
