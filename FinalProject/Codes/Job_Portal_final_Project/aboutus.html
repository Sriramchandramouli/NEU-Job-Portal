About Us

    Over the last 20 years, Northeastern Online Job portal has been at the forefront of innovation in the recruitment space. From our earliest days as a pioneer in job board technology to our current leadership in HR software as a service, we have made it our business to help job seekers and employers around the world connect anytime, anywhere.

  • Our Mission

    CareerBuilder's mission is to empower employment. We are striving to organize all the world's human capital data and make it meaningful for society.
