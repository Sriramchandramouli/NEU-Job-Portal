An invoice will be auto generated and a payment will be made to the customer by JobBoard.com. negative, then the customer owes JobBoard.com money. An invoice and statement will be sent to the customer and payment will be taken. There will be a total charge of £5 per month to the Client for Sterling eCommerce transactions. NETWORK

It is compulsory that all JobBoard.com Websites are members of the Network. There is no charge for membership to the Network. The Website shall display all jobs from the Network relevant to the Website audience, as set by the Client via the Administration Portal. The Client shall be eligible for receipt of an application fee for each Qualified Application made through the Website to a Network job. Application fees are paid at Market Rate, as fairly determined by JobBoard.com. Application fees are variable, and may alter per application. All jobs posted to the Website will be distributed to the Network with a Network Budget of 50% of the job purchase price. Any Network member with a corresponding audience base is eligible to advertise the End User's job. All applications will be made through the Client's website. Jobs may receive applications from the Network, and for which an application fee will be paid by the Client. The fee shall be taken from the Network Budget. At the point that the Network Budget is depleted, no further applications will be purchased from the Network, and the Client will not be liable for any further Network costs. Unspent Network Budget is released and made available for payment to the Client at the first monthly statement following the expiry of the given job. Any fraudulent applications found to be made by the Client to Network jobs will result in immediate suspension of the Website and Termination of the contract.
